\begin{abstract}

This project aims to develop a platform that integrates Blockchain technology and the Internet of Things (IoT) to manage the frozen food supply chain, with a primary focus on real-time temperature monitoring and recording. The developed system features automated alerts when product temperatures deviate from specified ranges and enables transparent, secure data traceability to reduce frozen food loss caused by improper temperatures during transportation.

The system comprises key components: product data recording by personnel; the implementation of IoT sensors to measure and transmit encrypted temperature data to a central system; a Blockchain-based real-time tracking system for automated data verification and logging; and an interface for officials to monitor data, analyze product status, and receive notifications for anomalies.

Furthermore, this project focuses on studying and testing the efficiency of Blockchain and IoT in enhancing frozen food supply chain management processes. It is anticipated that this innovation will increase transparency, minimize losses, and strengthen confidence in the quality of frozen food products throughout the entire supply chain.
\end{abstract}
